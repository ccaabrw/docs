To turn off the new Bing sidebar in Edge:

Add key:
HKCU:\Software\Policies\Mcrosoft\Edge\

Add value:
HubSidebarEnabled
REG_DWORD
0x0

Description of settings:
https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/deployedge/microsoft-edge-policies#hubssidebarenabled
https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/DeployEdge/microsoft-edge-sidebar
